• Twister OS Web Apps Launcher


    Web Apps are automatically updated.

    1) If you would like a shortcut for an app on your desktop please right click on your desktop and choose create launcher
    2) Fill in the Name of the App
    3) Click the folder icon under command section and navigate to /home/pi/WebApps/webapps/scripts and choose the app script you want.
    4) Click the icon button and under select icon from choose Image Files and navigate to /home/pi/WebApps/webapps/icons and choose the app icon you want.
    5) Click Create and your icon should appear on your desktop.
